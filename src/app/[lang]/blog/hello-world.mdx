Welcome to my new digital identity.

When building this site, I decided to ignore the noise and focus strictly on the **KISS** principle (Keep It Simple, Stupid).

## Why Simple is Better

By keeping the markdown clean and using `@tailwindcss/typography`, I can focus entirely on writing. No complex `<div className="flex">` tags injected into my posts. Just pure, unadulterated content.

1. It improves my writing speed.
2. It maximizes **E-E-A-T** for Google.
3. It ensures maximum performance on Cloudflare Edge.

Here is a blockquote to test the styling:

> "Complexity is the enemy of execution."
